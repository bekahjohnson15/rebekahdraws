About Me Resume Biography

She has worked at her local Sheetz for three years. She enjoyed working with her coworkers to create a clean and fully-stocked environment for full customer satisfaction.

An Early Elegance is the name of a tea stand in a local market. It has been a muse of Rebekah's for a few years now. The current website is not up-to-date. Rebekah is gathering material to propose making the owner a new website.

She owns a 1994 Pontiac Grand Am SE. It became a pet project to work on with her father, and she is very proud of it.

Rebekah has 10 cats. The oldest cat is 20 years old!

Due to it being a rural area, people drop off cats at local farms who often are not equipped to take care of the amount of cats released. We have taken in many over the years, and TNR as many as we can. All members of the family pitch in when taking care of the kittens, from socialization to feeding and medical needs.

Rebekah has several chickens

She has enjoyed playing Dungeons and Dragons, a tabletop roleplaying game, with her friends. The game involves creating characters, rolling dice, and creating scenarios. There is math, strategy, and social aspects to the game. She also draws characters in photoshop in her free time. Sayras is the character Rebekah is currently playing. She is a half-elf Eldritch Knight fighter, who is a former part of the Waterdeep Military branches in her campaign.

She enjoys playing video games. She is particularly good at Tetris, accomplishing “Tetris Maximus” (beating all other players) in Tetris 99.
