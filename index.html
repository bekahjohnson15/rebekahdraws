Wait a minute, who are you?

My name is Rebekah Johnson! This website is a showcase of some of my accomplishments and artworks completed over the past few years. My hobbies, interests, and history are also displayed here as well.



As a Harrisburg Area Community College Graduate, I am looking forward to entering the workforce. While studying to get my AAS in Web Development and Design, I have gained experience with HTML, CSS, SQL, C#, JavaScript, and products in the Adobe Creative Cloud. Social media literacy and being able to adapt to new applications are important technology skills I have.

I have worked at my local Sheetz for three years. I enjoyed working with my coworkers to create a clean and fully-stocked environment for full customer satisfaction. I have been described as an excellent team player. Through my experience as a third shift supervisor, I learned the importance of communication, a positive attitude, and keeping a cool head. Running a store taught me to come up with creative solutions to problems that may arise during a shift. It also was a good lesson in balancing and managing workloads. I attended college and balanced a third shift supervisor job, and elected to demote in order to focus on my last year of college. I have worked both first and third shift as a regular employee.



Some Interesting Things

I graduated New Oxford High School in 2018. My favorite classes and activities were drafting, 3d art, computer programming, and music, particularly the yearly musicals. I have volunteered time to my local church in my youth, helping in setting up events, minor construction, and child care. I have an interesting in acting and animation. I am part of an acting agency and participate in independed projects in my own time. I also have experience live-streaming online.
